About Me

Capturing sports history through my lens.

Hi, I’m Gavin Daly, a sports photographer dedicated to capturing historic sporting moments through my lens. For the past two years I have been working as a photo intern with Texas Tech Athletics, capturing moments from all sports. As an intern, I have had the opportunity to document the excitement of collegiate athletics from the sidelines. I shoot on cannon equipment and complete my workflow using Adobe Bridge, Lightroom Classic, and Photoshelter. From the first time I picked up a camera (three years ago), I have consistently improved in my ability to capture peak action and unforgettable moments in athletics.

I am always working to refine my craft and take the next step toward shooting at the professional level. One day, I hope to have the opportunity to complete my dream of being a photographer for a football club in England. Until then, I will continue to challenge myself in every creative way on the sidelines.
